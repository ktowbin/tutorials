Breaking the UVA 3-meter record

My greatest accomplishment thus far was breaking the UVA 3-meter record last year. Every day since I stepped on grounds, I had starred at the record board in hopes that someday my name would be on it. It was truly really exhilarating to finally do it last winter.

Looking back on it, I was really proud of how I handled each dive. During competition, it is important for me to stay in the present moment and avoid thinking about past or upcoming dives, and I was able to get into my zone and allow my body and mind to work in unison. It was such a fantastic feeling to look at the scoreboard and see all my hard work come to light.

My Dive List

The dives that I completed in this meet include:

  • 107c or front 3.5 somersaults tuck
  • 405c or inward 2.5 somersaults tuck
  • 5152d or front 2.5 somersaults pike with 1 twist
  • 205b or back 2.5 somersaults pike
  • 305b or reverse 2.5 somersaults pike
  • 5235d or back 1.5 somersaults free position with 1.5 twists
